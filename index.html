About

What if…

there was a high school focussed on high academic standards, and increased student engagement,

a high school focussed on critical thinking, leadership, and innovation,

a high school with experienced staff and a fresh approach to secondary education?

Welcome to... Cedar Ridge High School

Create

We can't teach tomorrow's leaders in yesterday's classrooms. Our high school offers students the opportunity to learn in a one of a kind modern learning commons. Resembling a modern start-up space, the learning commons is designed to facilitate communication, collaboration and creativity. The space allows students to easily work in large groups, small groups and independently. This 21st Century learning space fuels innovation, teamwork and critical thinking. At Cedar Ridge, we aren’t just preparing students for university; we are preparing them for life.

Innovate

"At Cedar Ridge, we never stop learning. Our students are taught in small groups and supported by caring qualified educators. We offer a one of a kind program that includes a strong leadership component. Our students, run their own student council, organize fundraising events in the community, write their own school policy documents and make important decisions about their school environment. To further stretch their leadership skills, students at Cedar Ridge are also offered the opportunity to participate in the Duke of Edinburgh Award.”

Educate

Cedar Ridge is committed to preparing students for the post-secondary program of their choice. Our program is 100% aligned with the Ontario curriculum and awards students an Ontario Secondary School Diploma. By providing students choice and voice, students at Cedar Ridge immerse themselves in projects and topics that are interesting and authentic. From mock elections, to engineering tasks, students at Cedar Ridge are engaged and challenged. Students develop strong academic skills as well as transferable skills like team work, problem solving and time management.
