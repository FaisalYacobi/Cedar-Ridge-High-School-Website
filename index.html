About

Cedar Ridge High School is an independent high school in Kanata, Ontario offering grades 9 to 12. Our school is an extension of an elementary school with 40 years of experience in private education. Our school offers students an opportunity to earn an Ontario Secondary School Diploma with a focus on leadership, innovation, student engagement and academics.

Mission

The successful adults of tomorrow will require a much broader and deeper high school program than the previous generation. At Cedar Ridge High School we know the world is changing, that's why we are changing education.

Our Students' Vision

"Cedar Ridge High School is a welcoming school that exceeds academic standards and encourages innovative learning. Cedar Ridge High School focuses on enrichment and student centered design with a personal connection between students and educators. Cedar Ridge provides a safe and creative environment to allow students to succeed throughout the future. Cedar Ridge provides the necessary tools for students to be successful members of the 21st century.‚Äù

Learning Environment

We can't teach tomorrow's leaders in yesterday's classrooms. Our high school offers students the opportunity to learn in a one of a kind modern learning commons. Resembling a modern start-up space, the learning commons is designed to facilitate communication, collaboration and creativity. The space allows students to easily work in large groups, small groups and independently. This 21st Century learning space fuels innovation, teamwork and critical thinking.
